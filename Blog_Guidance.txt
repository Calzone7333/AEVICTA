LOAN APPLICATION - BLOG MANAGEMENT GUIDE
========================================

1. HOW TO ACCESS THE BLOG MANAGER
---------------------------------
1. Go to your Admin Dashboard login page (e.g., https://aevicta/login).
2. Log in using your admin credentials.
3. On the left-hand sidebar, click on "Manage Blogs" (the icon that looks like a document).


2. HOW TO CREATE A NEW BLOG
---------------------------
Once you are on the "Manage Blogs" page, you will see a form titled "Compose New Blog". Fill in the following details:

- Blog Title: The main heading of your blog post (e.g., "Business Loans in Chennai").
- Blog Image: This is the MAIN image that appears at the top of the blog. Click "Choose File" to select an image from your computer.
- Category: The category of the blog (e.g., Business, Finance).
- Author: The name of the person writing the blog.
- Excerpt: A short 1-2 sentence summary of the blog. This shows up on the main blogs list page.


3. HOW TO WRITE THE CONTENT (HTML SUPPORT)
------------------------------------------
The "Content" box supports HTML, which means you can format your text exactly how you want it. 

Basic Formatting Rules:
- Paragraphs: Wrap your text in <p> and </p>. 
  Example: <p>This is a paragraph.</p>
- Bold Text: Use <b> and </b>.
  Example: <p>This is <b>important</b>.</p>
- Line Breaks: If you want to skip a line without creating a new paragraph, use <br>
- Headings: Use <h3> and </h3> for sub-headings.
  Example: <h3>Eligibility Criteria</h3>

FULL EXAMPLE OF BLOG CONTENT:
You can simply copy and paste this example into the "Content" box and change the text:

<h3>Why Get a Business Loan in Chennai?</h3>
<p>Chennai is rapidly growing, and businesses need capital to expand. A <b>business loan</b> can help you scale operations, buy new equipment, or increase inventory.</p>

<p>Here are the top reasons to apply:</p>
<p>
  1. Low Interest Rates<br>
  2. Flexible Repayment<br>
  3. Quick Approval
</p>

<h3>Eligibility Criteria</h3>
<p>To apply, you must have a registered business with at least <b>2 years</b> of operation history.</p>

4. HOW TO ADD IMAGES INSIDE THE BLOG CONTENT
--------------------------------------------
If you want to add an image in the middle of your text (not just the main top image):
1. Place your typing cursor inside the "Content" box exactly where you want the image to appear.
2. Click the small orange "Upload & Insert Image" button located just above the text box.
3. Select an image from your computer.
4. The system will automatically upload it and insert the correct HTML code (e.g., <img src="..." />) into your content!


5. EDITING AND DELETING BLOGS
-----------------------------
To Edit an existing blog:
1. Look at the table of existing blogs on the right side.
2. Click the blue "Edit" icon (pencil) next to the blog you want to change.
3. The form on the left will automatically fill with the existing content.
4. Make your changes. You can also click "Remove Image" to delete the main image.
5. Click "Update Blog Post".

To Delete an existing blog:
1. Click the red "Trash" icon next to the blog in the table.
2. Confirm the deletion.


Congratulations! You are now ready to manage your blogs like a pro!
